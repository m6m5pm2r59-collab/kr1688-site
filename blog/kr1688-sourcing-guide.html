The Language Barrier Problem

Here's what happens when a Western buyer tries to use 1688 alone:

  • The entire platform is in Chinese — no English version exists
  • Supplier chat is in Mandarin, often with local dialect and industry shorthand
  • Product specifications, certifications, and shipping terms are in Chinese
  • Payment gateways (Alipay, WeChat Pay) require a Chinese bank account
  • Return and dispute processes are conducted entirely in Chinese

Google Translate helps with product pages, but it fails miserably with negotiation, quality discussions, and logistics coordination — the moments where deals are won or lost.
